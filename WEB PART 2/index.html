About:

Here is our story:

  • Masinqobe Foundation

    Masinqobe means 'let us succeed/overcome', which is exactly what this Foundation is for. Our mission is to equip youth and communities with essential life skills,career guidance,and support,fostering personal growth,social responsibility,and economic empowerment. Our vision is to assist in creating a thriving society where young people and communities have skills, confidence and opportunities to reach thier full potential, contributing to a brighter future for all.

Life Skills

Hlanganani Primary

Hlanganani Primary recently had the honor of participating in Eskom's Science Expo of which thier grade 7 team won first place. According to the principle these are the fruits of Masinqobe's Life skills project that has taught thier learners to be innovative and to reach for every opportunity.

Community Cleanup

Murchison cleanup

Everyone has a right to a clean and safe enviroment; as Masinqobe we pride ourselves in lending a helping hand to our surrounding community(Murchison) to help achieve a clean and safe enviroment.

Career Exhibitions

South Coast Sports and Leisure Center

In partnership with Department of Education ,we hold an annual career exhibition for high schools all over South Coast where they can be eduacated on different courses and careers by various institues of higher education.

Teen Mom Supprt

Nokuthula Mbotho's Story

Nokuthula is a teen mom from a poor background.She was on the verge of dropping out school after she had her baby but she heard about Masinqobe and reached out.She is the reason why Masinqobe started a teen mom suppprt group so that ,just like Nokuthula,teen moms do not jeopardize thier futures potentially ruining their child's future in the process.

Girl in Sports

Gamalakhe sports center

Various township schools do not offer extra mural activities due to lack of resources, this foundation has taken the initiative to create a program targeting young girls to play sports and enhance thier athletic abilities.

Food Packages

Handed out at Genesis centers.

A lot of people from the surrounding rural areas cannot afford basic needs such as food to sustain thier families. Food packages are Handed out at Genesis centers every Sunday. This helps to provide food to those who are struggling.
